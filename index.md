### About Me
Hi! I'm a M.S. student in the **Integrated Circuits and Systems (EC78) track at the **University of California, San Diego (UCSD), Fall 2026. I earned my B.Eng. in Electrical Engineering at East China Normal University (ECNU), Shanghai, China in 2026. 

My research interests include **AI Hardware accelerators and architectures**, Digital Integrated Circuits Design. I work on the end-to-end flow for AI hardware systems, bridging the gap between algorithms and physical implementation—from neural network modeling to RTL design and FPGA prototyping.
