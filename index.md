### About Me
Hi! I'm a M.S. student in the **Integrated Circuits and Systems (EC78) track at the **University of California, San Diego (UCSD), Fall 2026. I earned my B.Eng. in Electrical Engineering at East China Normal University (ECNU), Shanghai, China in 2026.
